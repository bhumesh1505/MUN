Message from Secretary General
AXIS’19 takes immense pleasure to announce the organisation of the AXIS MUN, for the first time ever in VNIT. Premiering this February is a legacy, a pride, a zeal that has been carried over ever since the start of this wonder, which has been inspiring, lives throughout the world, all for a better cause. A Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations in which participants assume the role of diplomats from different countries and deliberate over international issues. While the direct lessons from MUNs are manifold, the indirect benefits are the ones that stay with you for a lifetime. The ability to dissect and analyse seemingly complex concepts, to acknowledge and understand the multifaceted nature of problems, and most importantly, to actively listen to friends and foes alike, is what shelves MUNs in the “invaluable” section of the library that is life.So this year, gear up for an experience like never before, where you magnify views from all directions of the room and in the journey solidify your own. The AXIS team hopes to receive humongous interest and participation for when it’s about modelling the future with our very own hands, who wouldn’t came and delegate their lives out. Yours diplomatically,(GS)
Message from Director General
“More than ever before in human history, we share a common destiny. We can master it only if we face it together. And that, my friends, is why we have the United Nations.” -Kofi Annan . The world has changed its course in politics many times. Foreign policies of nations have taken steep turns, enemies have become allies and allies have become enemies. However, as Kofi Annan beautifully said that our final destiny is same. History will testify that our interdependence only increases with time. It is easy to adopt and enact “beggar be thy neighbour” policies. In fact, many countries have adopted this system. What is tough is to act in a manner in which everybody’s interests are been taken care of. And in such situations what should be the role of our young minds, the probable leaders of tomorrow? It is now time to find the hidden diplomat under us The Legacy is the footprint of the visionaries who have given their blood, sweat, and tears to build this conference from the ground up. It is the sense of fraternity that we’ve built through our inclusive approach to success. It is the promise that the bonds you make and the knowledge you gain will stay with you for a lifetime, irrespective of what capacity you participate in. And thus I am proud to present you the very 1st edition of AXIS MUN 2018 as the Director General. (Director General)

NAME HERE

The Lok Sabha (House of the People) is the lower house of India's bicameral Parliament. Members of the Lok Sabha are elected by adult universal suffrage and a first-past-the-post system to represent their respective constituencies, and they hold their seats for five years or until the body is dissolved by the President on the advice of the council of ministers. The house meets in the Lok Sabha Chambers of the Sansad Bhavan in New Delhi. The maximum strength of the house is 552. In TechMUN the main motive of Loksabha is to discuss and implement the improving developments in India.

FAQ


What is MUN (Model United Nations)?

Model UN or MUN is basically an academic simulation of of the United Nations that aims to encourage students to discuss about the major issues concerning the world and international politics.

What is an Agenda?

Agenda refers to the particle issue on which the committee would be discussing. It is the primary reason for assembling.

Who can register for AXIS MUN?

Participation in AXIS MUN is open to working professionals and students pursuing their undergraduate, post graduate and PhD. School students cannot participate.

What is the registration process for AXIS MUN?

When will the allotments be released?

It would be released at the end of January month when the registrations are closed.
